Cats Drop

Cats Drop is a puzzle game where identical cats are merged to get the most valued cat. The game introduces different-size and colored cats that come together in a very simple yet strategic game that requires correct placement to avoid overflow of the measuring cup. As the challenge heightens, quick thinking brings in high scores in the game, making it interesting for both puzzle lovers and cat enthusiasts alike.

How to Play Cats Drop Game

  • Use your mouse to drag the cat to the desired position over the measuring cup.
  • Left-click to drop the cat into the cup.
  • Place cats of the same size and color next to each other to merge them up into one bigger cat.
  • Plan ahead to avoid an overflow of unmerged cats into the measuring cup.
  • Keep merging to make the largest cat and top the score.

Controls

Control with the mouse.

FAQs

Q. What will happen in case the measuring cup overflows?
A. If the cup overflows, the game is over and the final of your scores.

Q. Can Cats Drop be played on mobile?
A. Cats Drop can run on desktop and mobile, which enables you to play at any place.
